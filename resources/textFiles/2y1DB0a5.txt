Once your card runs out of money, you’ll have to load more funds on it before you can use it again. And since you load the card with your own money, there’s no balance that you owe and have to pay back.What are the benefits of a prepaid card?A prepaid card has the convenience and flexibility of a standard credit card, but without the stress of remembering to pay it off each month or paying interest. Your BMO Prepaid Mastercard also comes with security features, making it much safer to use than cash.How do I load funds onto my card?Do you have a BMO chequing or savings account? If you do, simply transfer funds to your BMO Prepaid Mastercard account through online or mobile banking. Your funds will be available shortly.If you don’t have a BMO account, you can load any amount under $1,000 on your BMO Prepaid Mastercard directly from any bank account from most financial institutions, just as you would making a bill payment.Register your BMO Prepaid Mastercard as a bill payee.Load money via bill payment through online banking, ATM, phone banking or at a branch.
