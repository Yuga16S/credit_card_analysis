Are you an international student? You can apply for a BMO credit card by visiting a branch.Have reached the age of majority in the province in which you live (18 years in AB, MB, ON, PE, QC and SK. 19 years in all other provinces)Earn annual income from employment, scholarship/grant, student loan or family allowanceWhat are the benefits of a student credit card?Along with convenience, there are a number of reasons why applying for a student credit card makes financial sense:A student card is generally easier to qualify for than other credit cards.You’ll start building a credit history. By using your card and paying if off every month, you’ll show you’re responsible with credit, making it easier to qualify for a loan, line of credit or mortgage in the future.You’ll earn Reward Miles every time you spend.How can I find out how many AIR MILES Reward Miles I have?You can check your AIR MILES Summary on your Mastercard monthly statement. It will display the amount of Reward Miles you got on purchases for the stated time period but won’t include AIR MILES Reward Miles from bonus or promotional offers.You can also see your total number of Reward Miles when you sign in to BMO Online or Mobile banking, on your AIR MILES Collector Summary statement, and through airmiles.ca.What can I use AIR MILES Reward Miles for?When it comes to using AIR MILES Reward Miles, there’s so much to choose from. There are over 800 products and services that you can use Miles for, including merchandise, travel, gift cards, leisure and entertainment items. So whether you’re looking for a new TV, blender, toy or a night at the movies, find something great with the AIR MILES Reward Program.Get all the details of the Reward options by visiting airmiles.ca.Where can I find out how many AIR MILES Reward Miles I need for a flight?Find how close you are to your dream vacation by visiting airmiles.ca and click the Rewards tab. Under Dream Rewards , select Travel and check out the options for Flights.If you’re already an AIR MILES Collector, you can search specific flights and times to see exactly how many Miles you need. If you’re not a Collector yet, click the See where you can fly link to get an estimate of the how many Miles you need for a round trip flight.Help!
